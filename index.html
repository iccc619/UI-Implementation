AustralianLight and Gallery 6 31

Tuesday, May 21, 2019

With the official opening of Brisbane's newest Art Gallery Gallery 6 31, we now have samples of our Canvas, Floating Acrylic and traditional Matte & Frame image presentations on display.

Gallery 6 31 hosts many popular Australian artists and is open Monday to Friday 9am to 4pm at 6/31 Thompson Street, Bowen Hills Brisbane.
